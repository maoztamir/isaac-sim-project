SCENE ID: WAREHOUSE_DOOR_IDLE_003
TITLE: Door Idle While Open

INT. WAREHOUSE – LOADING DOCK 2 – DAY

PURPOSE
Show an anomalous idle state: dock door is open, trailer is attached, pallets are
present — but no loading activity is occurring. The scene should feel like
something should be happening, but isn't.

CAMERA
Shot type: Static, dock-facing
Frame: Door 2 full width; pallets near entrance foreground; staging visible behind
Focus: Empty threshold — door open, no forklift engaging
Lens / height: 24–35mm equivalent, eye level or slightly elevated  [MANUAL ADJUSTMENT]

LAYOUT / ZONES
Z1: Door 2 loading zone (active door, trailer attached)
Z2: Entrance apron (where pallets sit)
Z3: Staging area beyond (background reference)

ACTORS
P0, P1, P2: pallets near door entrance, unattended
FL0: forklift (optional) — background pass, does not engage

INITIAL STATE
t=0.0
- Door 2: open
- Trailer: attached at dock
- P0, P1, P2: placed near door entrance
- No forklift inside loading zone or approaching

TIMELINE / ACTION BEATS
00.0–03.0
- Nothing happens.
- Door open. Pallets present. Trailer waiting. No movement.

03.0–06.0
- FL0 (optional) passes through background without entering loading zone.
- No engagement with door or pallets.

06.0–08.0
- Still no loading activity.
- Door remains open. Pallets untouched. Trailer idle.

08.0+
- Scene holds. door_idle_duration continues accumulating.

COORDINATION RULES
- FL0 must NOT enter the loading zone at any point.
- No forklift may approach or stop at Door 2 during the scene.
- Pallets remain stationary throughout.

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- 8 s elapse with door open and no loading activity.
- door_idle_duration alert fires at or before configured threshold.
- Trailer and pallets visually present throughout.
- Optional FL0 pass reads as background activity, not engagement.

FAILURE CONDITIONS
- Any forklift enters or approaches the loading zone.
- Pallets move or disappear.
- Door closes during scene.
- FL0 pass is ambiguous — looks like it could be starting to load.

OBSERVABILITY
Log:
- door_idle_duration (seconds since door opened with no forklift inside)
- dock_occupied (should remain False throughout)
- forklift_proximity_to_door (nearest FL distance to door threshold)
- idle_alert_fired timestamp

Mobile alert context:
  "Door 7 idle — 8 min | no loading"
