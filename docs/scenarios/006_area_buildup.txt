SCENE ID: WAREHOUSE_AREA_BUILDUP_006
TITLE: Staging Area Build-Up

INT. WAREHOUSE – STAGING AREA / LOADING ZONES – OVERHEAD – DAY

PURPOSE
Show staging saturation: forklifts arrive in staging faster than the dock releases
them, causing a visible pile-up of vehicles and pallets. Pallets are not stacked
cleanly — the area looks packed and disorganised. Forward progress stalls.

CAMERA
Shot type: Overhead or elevated south-facing wide
Frame: Full staging area + all dock loading zones
Focus: Progressive forklift and pallet accumulation; density rising over time
Lens / height: Overhead, 5–8 m elevated  [MANUAL ADJUSTMENT]

LAYOUT / ZONES
Z1: Staging area (capacity: 6 forklifts)
Z2: Dock loading zones (slow-release bottleneck)
Z3: Shelf aisles (origin)

ACTORS
FL0–FL4: 5 forklifts, FSM-driven, full cycle

INITIAL STATE
t=0.0
- All dock doors: open
- FL0–FL4: distributed across shelf aisles and staging, cycling normally
- Dock release interval: 8 s between releases (intentionally slow)
- Loading duration per forklift: 12 s

TIMELINE / ACTION BEATS
00.0–30.0
- Forklifts pick pallets and arrive at staging progressively.
- Dock releases slowly — forklifts accumulate faster than dock clears.
- Staging occupancy climbs: 1 → 2 → 3.
- Pallets visible in staging — scattered, not stacked cleanly.

30.0–60.0
- Staging continues filling: 3 → 4 → 5.
- Forklifts that arrive hold positions with pallets.
- Dock continues slow release. Packed staging visible from above.

60.0+
- buildup_threshold fires when staging occupancy >= 6.
- All remaining forklifts queue and wait.
- Forward progress visually stalled. Scene holds at saturation.

COORDINATION RULES
- Dock release interval must remain at 8 s — do not accelerate during scene.
- Forklifts must not skip staging and go directly to dock.
- Staging forklifts hold in place — no return to shelves while waiting.
- buildup_threshold fires at occupancy >= 6, not before.

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- Staging occupancy reaches capacity (6) by ~60 s.
- buildup_threshold event fires at or before 60 s.
- Visual density in staging is clearly congested — packed forklifts and pallets visible.
- Dock loading activity continues throughout (dock itself is not stalled).

FAILURE CONDITIONS
- Forklifts return to shelves instead of holding in staging.
- Dock release interval accelerates, clearing staging too fast.
- buildup_threshold never fires.
- Staging looks sparse or orderly when alert fires.

OBSERVABILITY
Log:
- staging_occupancy per frame
- buildup_threshold_fired timestamp
- dock_release_count over time
- forklift_wait_duration per FL (seconds held in staging)

Mobile alert context:
  "Staging congestion — 7 min | 12–15 pallets"
