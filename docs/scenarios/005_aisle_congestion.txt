SCENE ID: WAREHOUSE_AISLE_CONGESTION_005
TITLE: Aisle Congestion

INT. WAREHOUSE – NARROW SHELF AISLE – DAY

PURPOSE
Show density-driven speed reduction and hesitation in a narrow shelf aisle when
too many forklifts funnel into the same lane. Movement exists but is slowed,
inefficient, and visually tense — one forklift pauses, another hesitates at the
entry, none can commit to full forward speed.

CAMERA
Shot type: Bird's-eye or elevated end-of-aisle static
Frame: Full aisle length; rack walls on both sides visible
Focus: Forklift density, speed reduction, hesitation and near-blocking moments
Lens / height: Overhead wide or elevated end-of-aisle  [MANUAL ADJUSTMENT]

LAYOUT / ZONES
Z1: Target shelf aisle (congestion zone)
Z2: Aisle entry points (where forklifts queue)
Z3: Adjacent aisles (visible but not targeted)

ACTORS
FL0–FL5: 6 forklifts, all targeting the same narrow aisle
          Loading duration: 6 s (short, so forklifts cycle back quickly)

INITIAL STATE
t=0.0
- FL0–FL5: near south wall or aisle entries, all assigned to same aisle
- Aisle clear at start
- Short loading duration ensures rapid re-entry

TIMELINE / ACTION BEATS
00.0–15.0
- Forklifts begin funneling into the target aisle in staggered order.
- First 1–2 enter; remaining approach and queue at entry.

15.0–30.0
- Aisle fills. Speeds drop as density rises.
- One forklift pauses mid-aisle; another reroutes or holds at entry.
- Visible hesitation — forklifts cannot commit to full forward movement.

30.0+
- Congestion sustained. All vehicles at or approaching aisle at <30% max speed.
- congestion_alert fires per forklift inside the congested zone.
- Movement exists but reads as slowed and inefficient.

COORDINATION RULES
- All forklifts target the same aisle — no FSM rerouting to other aisles.
- Minimum in-aisle separation: 1.0 m.
- At most 2 forklifts may be fully inside the aisle simultaneously; others queue at entry.
- Speed reduction is automatic from forklift density logic — do not hardcode.

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- All 6 forklifts reach or queue for the target aisle by 30 s.
- Speed of forklifts inside aisle falls below 30% max by 30 s.
- congestion_alert fires for each forklift in the congested zone.
- Hesitation / near-blocking moment visible in viewport.

FAILURE CONDITIONS
- Forklifts self-reroute to adjacent aisles, dispersing the congestion.
- Forklifts move at full speed through the aisle — no visible slowdown.
- congestion_alert never fires.
- Forklifts clip racks or each other.

OBSERVABILITY
Log:
- aisle_occupancy (forklifts inside target aisle) per frame
- forklift_speed_in_aisle per FL (m/s)
- congestion_alert per FL (timestamp + FL id)
- min_FL_separation_in_aisle
