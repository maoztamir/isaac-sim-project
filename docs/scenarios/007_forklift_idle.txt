SCENE ID: WAREHOUSE_FORKLIFT_IDLE_007
TITLE: Forklift Idle – Subtle / Hidden

INT. WAREHOUSE – SIDE AISLE / LOW-TRAFFIC CORNER – DAY

PURPOSE
Show hidden inefficiency: a forklift sits idle in a low-visibility corner — not
clearly broken, not properly parked, not obviously on break. The contrast with
faint background activity makes the idle state feel like something is wrong, but
it's easy to miss from central operations.

CAMERA
Shot type: Fixed, slightly elevated — CCTV feel
Frame: Idle forklift in foreground; active main aisle faintly visible in background
Focus: FL0 FG / active area BG — contrast between idle and working is essential
Lens / height: Fixed elevated, 3–5 m, slight downward angle  [MANUAL ADJUSTMENT]

LAYOUT / ZONES
Z1: Side aisle / low-traffic corner (FL0 idle position)
Z2: Main aisle (background — light activity only)

ACTORS
FL0: forklift, idle, no pallets — in side aisle, not near docks or parking
FL1, FL2 (optional): background forklifts cycling in main aisle; not foregrounded

INITIAL STATE
t=0.0
- FL0: stationary in side aisle or low-traffic corner
- Position: not near docks, not in designated parking, not blocking a path
- FL1, FL2 (optional): cycling normally in background main aisle

TIMELINE / ACTION BEATS
00.0–03.0
- FL0 stationary. No movement. No pallet handling.
- Background forklifts (if present) pass through main aisle.

03.0–05.0
- FL0 makes a small positional micro-adjustment — slight wheel turn or minor shift.
- Does NOT look like departure or parking. Reads as an operator check, nothing more.

05.0–08.0
- FL0 returns to idle. Remains stationary.
- Background activity continues undisturbed.

08.0+
- idle_alert fires for FL0.
- Scene holds at full idle.

COORDINATION RULES
- FL0 must NOT be near docks, loading zones, or designated parking spots.
- FL0 must NOT look broken, intentionally parked, or clearly on break.
- Micro-adjustment at 3–5 s must not read as departure — operator fidget only.
- Background FLs must stay in background — they must not approach FL0.

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- idle_alert fires for FL0 at or after configured idle threshold.
- FL0 remains in side aisle / corner for full scene duration.
- Background activity visible but clearly separate from FL0.
- Scene reads as hidden inefficiency — not obvious at a glance.

FAILURE CONDITIONS
- FL0 looks broken or intentionally parked (defeats the "hidden" quality).
- FL0 departs or approaches a dock or active zone.
- Background forklifts draw attention away from FL0.
- FL0 idle position is too central — visible from main operations view.

OBSERVABILITY
Log:
- FL0_idle_duration (continuous seconds stationary)
- FL0_position (confirm side aisle / corner, not dock / main area)
- idle_alert_fired timestamp
- background_FL_count (confirm background activity present)

Mobile alert context:
  "Forklift idle — 9 min | side aisle"
