SCENE ID: WAREHOUSE_DOCK_QUEUE_001
TITLE: Dock Queue Forming

INT. WAREHOUSE – LOADING DOCK 2 / STAGING AREA – DAY

PURPOSE
Show queue formation behind an active dock-loading forklift.

CAMERA
Shot type: Static / wide operational shot
Frame: Door 2 centered; loading zone foreground; staging lanes visible behind
Focus: FL0 at dock, FL1 and FL2 queue behavior
Lens / height: 24–35mm equivalent, elevated 3–5 m  [MANUAL ADJUSTMENT]

LAYOUT / ZONES
Z1: Door 2 loading zone
Z2: Left staging area near Door 1
Z3: Center staging area near Door 2
Z4: Center dock queue lane

ACTORS
FL0: forklift, unloaded/working, dock operator
FL1: forklift, loaded, first queue entrant
FL2: forklift, loaded, second queue entrant
P0: pallet beside FL0 in loading zone

INITIAL STATE
t=0.0
- Door 2: open
- FL0: parked at center loading zone, mid-loading
- P0: in loading zone beside FL0
- FL1: left staging area, loaded, facing dock
- FL2: center staging area, loaded, facing dock

TIMELINE / ACTION BEATS
00.0–02.0
- FL1 drives from left staging area to center dock queue position.
- FL1 stops at Queue_Position_1.
- FL0 holds.
- FL2 holds.

02.0–06.0
- FL0 performs small forward/back micro-adjustments at dock.
- FL1 waits in queue.
- FL2 waits in staging.

06.0–08.0
- FL2 drives south from center staging area.
- FL2 stops behind FL1 at Queue_Position_2.
- FL0 continues micro-adjustments.
- FL1 holds.

08.0+
- FL0, FL1, FL2 hold final positions.

COORDINATION RULES
- Queue order: FL1 before FL2.
- FL2 must not enter queue until FL1 has stopped.
- Maintain minimum forklift spacing: 1.5–2.0 m.
- FL0 dock zone has priority; queued forklifts do not enter loading zone.

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- FL1 reaches first queue position by 2s.
- FL2 reaches second queue position by 8s.
- No collisions or overlap.
- Queue ordering is visually clear.
- Door 2 and loading activity remain visible throughout.

FAILURE CONDITIONS
- FL2 overtakes or blocks FL1.
- Any forklift clips pallet, door frame, or another forklift.
- Camera loses staging or queue context.
- FL0 micro-adjustments look like full departure instead of loading activity.

OBSERVABILITY
Log:
- queue_length over time
- FL1_wait_start
- FL2_wait_start
- min_distance_FL1_FL2
- dock_occupied_by_FL0
