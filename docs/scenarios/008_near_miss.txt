SCENE ID: WAREHOUSE_NEAR_MISS_008
TITLE: Near Miss – Forklift Reversing / Pedestrian

INT. WAREHOUSE – NARROW AISLE – DAY

PURPOSE
Show a quiet near-miss event: a forklift reversing with a loaded pallet (driver
attention directed backward/upward, away from travel path) and a pedestrian
walking in the same direction, unaware. Distance closes to under 1 m with no
collision and no dramatic stop — just closing proximity that should have been
caught by the system.

CAMERA
Shot type: Static, aisle-facing or slightly elevated overhead
Frame: Full aisle width; both actors visible simultaneously at all times
Focus: Closing gap between forklift rear and pedestrian
Lens / height: Elevated 3–4 m or end-of-aisle  [MANUAL ADJUSTMENT]

LAYOUT / ZONES
Z1: Target aisle (near-miss corridor)
Z2: Aisle entry (pedestrian approach)
Z3: Crossing point (where proximity peaks — <1 m)

ACTORS
FL0: forklift, reversing with loaded forks — driver looking back, away from pedestrian
P0: pedestrian, walking away from FL0 in same aisle — unaware, not looking back

INITIAL STATE
t=0.0
- FL0: at one end of aisle, loaded, about to begin reversing
- P0: already in aisle walking away from FL0, or entering from far end
- Both on same aisle axis; paths converge as FL0 reverses

TIMELINE / ACTION BEATS
00.0–03.0
- FL0 begins reversing — loaded forks raised, driver attention directed backward.
- P0 walks away down the aisle. Neither aware of the other.

03.0–06.0
- P0 continues walking, unaware of approaching forklift.
- FL0 continues reversing — no awareness of pedestrian in path.
- Gap closes progressively and visibly.

06.0–08.0
- Distance between FL0 rear and P0 drops below 1 m.
- proximity_alert fires.
- No collision. No sudden stop. No drama.
- Just closing distance — the problem is quiet.

08.0+
- Scene holds or actors pass without contact.

COORDINATION RULES
- FL0 must be reversing — not driving forward.
- FL0 forks must be loaded (obstructing driver's forward sightline).
- P0 must be walking away from FL0 (unaware) — not toward it.
- No physical contact at any point; proximity_alert fires before contact.
- Forklift-forklift fallback: if pedestrian actor unavailable, two forklifts on
  converging routes within NEAR_MISS_DIST (2.5 m), one above NEAR_MISS_SPEED_MIN.

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- proximity_alert fires when FL0–P0 distance < 1.0 m.
- No physical contact between FL0 and P0.
- Scene reads as a quiet, almost-missed event — not a dramatic stop.
- Both actors moving throughout (no pre-alert stop).

FAILURE CONDITIONS
- FL0 stops before reaching near-miss distance (too cautious — alert never approaches).
- P0 turns or stops — awareness breaks the "unaware" quality.
- Contact occurs (collision, not near-miss).
- Camera angle misses the closing gap — distance not readable in viewport.

OBSERVABILITY
Log:
- FL0_P0_distance per frame
- proximity_alert_fired timestamp (and distance value at trigger)
- FL0_speed at trigger
- FL0_reverse_flag (confirm reversing, not forward travel)
- P0_aware_flag (should remain False throughout)

Mobile alert context:
  "Forklift near miss — <1 m | Aisle 8"
