SCENE ID: WAREHOUSE_LOADING_PAUSE_004
TITLE: Loading Pause – Door Closure Mid-Cycle

INT. WAREHOUSE – DOCK DOORS / STAGING AREA – DAY

PURPOSE
Show operational disruption: a dock door closes unexpectedly mid-cycle, stalling
forklifts that were approaching and causing idle time to accumulate at the dock.
The cycle visibly resumes once the door reopens.

CAMERA
Shot type: Static, dock-facing or south-facing wide
Frame: Dock doors + staging area; forklifts visible at approach and hold positions
Focus: Door 2 closure event and forklift response
Lens / height: Wide angle, elevated 3–4 m  [MANUAL ADJUSTMENT]

LAYOUT / ZONES
Z1: Door 2 loading zone
Z2: Staging area (approach / hold positions)
Z3: Shelf aisles (origin of cycle)

ACTORS
FL0: forklift, FSM-driven, approaching dock when door closes
FL1: forklift, FSM-driven, in staging when door closes
FL2: forklift, FSM-driven, in shelf aisle when door closes

INITIAL STATE
t=0.0
- Door 2: open
- FL0, FL1, FL2: cycling normally — shelves → staging → dock
- Normal loading pace established; at least one load cycle already completed

TIMELINE / ACTION BEATS
00.0–30.0
- FL0, FL1, FL2 shuttle pallets in normal FSM cycle.
- Dock, staging, and aisle zones all active.

30.0
- Door 2 closes (gate descends).
- FL0 (approaching dock) stops advancing — holds position.
- FL1 (in staging) holds. FL2 (completing shelf pick) arrives at staging and holds.

30.0–50.0
- All forklifts hold near dock approach or staging.
- No loading progress. Idle time accumulates.
- door_idle_accumulation counter runs per forklift.

50.0
- Door 2 reopens.
- Forklifts advance to dock in queue order. Normal cycle resumes.

COORDINATION RULES
- No forklift may enter the loading zone while door is closed.
- Forklifts hold at staging or approach — do not return to shelves.
- Resumption follows queue position at time of closure (FL0 first, then FL1, FL2).

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- Door closes at 30 s, visible in viewport.
- All forklifts stop advancing within 2 s of closure.
- idle_alert fires for each forklift during the 30–50 s window.
- Door reopens at 50 s and cycle resumes in correct order.

FAILURE CONDITIONS
- Any forklift enters loading zone while door is closed.
- Forklifts drift back to shelves instead of holding.
- Door close/open not clearly visible in viewport.
- Resumption order is random instead of queue-based.

OBSERVABILITY
Log:
- door_state (open/closed) over time
- forklift_hold_start per FL (timestamp each stops due to closure)
- idle_accumulation_per_FL (seconds waiting at door)
- door_reopen_timestamp
- idle_alert_fired timestamp

Mobile alert context:
  "Door 12 inactive — 20 min | no loading"
